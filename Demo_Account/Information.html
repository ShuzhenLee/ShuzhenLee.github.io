You will be presented with real historical data about the performance of two funds over 45 periods. The details of the funds are as follows:

Conventional Fund:

This fund offers exposure to the largest U.S. companies, providing a portfolio that spans multiple sectors and industries. It is designed for investors who prioritize broad market performance and efficiency at a low cost. It does NOT exclude equities which operate in controversial industries such as fossil fuel, tobacco, and weapons.


Sustainable Fund:

This fund emphasizes investments in U.S. companies that demonstrate strong environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices. It is managed to select companies that meet high sustainability standards, making it suitable for investors seeking to align their portfolios with responsible investment principles while pursuing competitive financial returns.

Both funds are currently active in the financial market but have been anonymized for the purpose of the study. All data shown in this study represent real historical performance.
