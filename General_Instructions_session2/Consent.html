You are being asked to take part in a research study conducted by Dr. Peiran Jiao (Maastricht University), Drs. Shuzhen Li (Utrecht University), and Dr. Yilong Xu (Utrecht University).

The purpose of this research is to better understand how you make decisions and how you form beliefs about decisions. The study will take approximately 15 minutes to complete. The study includes tasks and a short follow-up questionnaire. You will receive clear instructions and will be provided with full and accurate information on how your decisions will influence your payment. None of the data you provide in this study will be linked to any information that could identify you as a participant. In the future, only anonymized data may be shared.

Your participation is completely voluntary. You can agree to take part and later change your mind. Your decision will not be held against you. If you have questions or concerns, please write to the research team at s.li3@uu.nl.
