Welcome to the Study!

In this study, you will make investment decisions involving two funds. The study consists of four stages, each with specific instructions provided at the beginning of the respective stage.

Your final payoff will consist of:

1. Completion Fee: A guaranteed completion fee of 1.5 GBPs.

2. Earnings from the Investment Task: More details will be provided in the task instructions.

3. Correct Answer Bonus: For some questions in the questionnaire, you will receive bonuses for correct answers.

All earnings in this study are expressed in Experimental Currency Unit (ECU). Your final payment will be converted to GBP at an exchange rate of 500 ECUs = 1 GBP.
